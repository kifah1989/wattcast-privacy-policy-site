Introduction

Welcome to WattCast ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application WattCast (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

1. Location Data

We collect your device's location data to provide accurate solar energy predictions:

  • What we collect: GPS coordinates (latitude and longitude)
  • Why we collect it: To calculate solar position, sunrise/sunset times, and fetch local weather data for your specific location
  • How it's used: Location data is sent to weather API services to retrieve current conditions and forecasts
  • Storage: We do not store your location data on our servers. Location is only used in real-time during app usage

2. Device Information

We may collect certain device information for app functionality:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version
  • Language preferences

3. Usage Data

We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app:

  • App crashes and error reports
  • Feature usage patterns (anonymized)

Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

Weather API Services

  • We use weather APIs to fetch weather data based on your location
  • These services receive your coordinates to provide weather forecasts
  • Please refer to the weather service's privacy policy for their data practices

Google AdMob

  • We display advertisements through Google AdMob
  • AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads
  • For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy

Google Play Services

  • The App uses Google Play Services for core functionality
  • See Google Play Services Privacy Policy

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide Core Functionality: Calculate solar energy output predictions based on your location and weather conditions
  • Improve the App: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and fix bugs
  • Display Advertisements: Show relevant ads through Google AdMob
  • Customer Support: Respond to your requests and provide assistance

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • All data transmission uses secure HTTPS connections
  • We do not store personal location data on external servers
  • Settings are stored locally on your device only

Data Retention

  • Location Data: Not retained; used only during active app sessions
  • Settings: Stored locally on your device until you uninstall the app
  • Usage Analytics: Retained in anonymized form for up to 12 months

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request information about data we collect
  • Deletion: Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data
  • Location Control: Revoke location permissions through your device settings
  • Ad Personalization: Opt out of personalized ads through your device's ad settings

Children's Privacy

WattCast is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.
